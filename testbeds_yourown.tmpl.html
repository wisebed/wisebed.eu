Testbeds
Run your own Testbed

During the WISEBED project we developed a testbed management software called "Testbed Runtime" (TR). TR allows you to set up your own testbed within minutes.

You can use it for your local desktop testbed or your research institutions testbed. It even can scale up to thousands of nodes! In fact, TR has also been used for experimentation and administration of the SmartSantander smart city deployment!

Please check the Testbed Runtime homepage at https://github.com/itm/testbed-runtime to learn more.
