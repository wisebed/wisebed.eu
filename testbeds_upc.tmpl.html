Testbed Description

The testbed at the Technical University of Catalonia is distributed around different rooms at the Software Department (building Omega, 2nd. floor), located in Campus Nord. The testbed consists currently of 10 iSense nodes in 5 rooms.

The characteristics of UPC testbed nodes (iSense nodes by coalesenses) are the following:

  • Jennic 32-bit RISC Controller, 16MHz (JN5139R1)
  • IEEE 802.15.4 compliant radio, 250 kbit/s, hardware AES Encryption
  • High accuracy (20ppm) real time clock
  • Software controllable voltage regulator
  • High-gain SMA antenna
  • Fixed Power Supply via USB Interface

Additionally, and independently of the testbed itself, there is a dedicated pair of iSense sensors. One of them is equiped with a solar module and temperature and light sensors. Its function is to gather following data:

  • Controller voltage
  • Supply voltage
  • Battery voltage
  • Battery charge
  • Battery current
  • Battery temperature
  • Charge status
  • Power source (basically wether there's solar power available)
  • Environmental temperature
  • Environmental light

It samples all this data every minute and sends it to the second sensor by radio. This sensor is connected to a computer via USB. It formats and sends the data to the computer through the USB port.

This data is available through the following URL: http://pctic0801.lsi.upc.edu:8080/
